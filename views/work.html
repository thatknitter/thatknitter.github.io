HealthFULL Eating

HealthFull Eating is a site I started work on in 2013. It focuses on educating families on food and the "whys" of nutrition. Instead of saying "eat this, because it's good for you, and don't ask questions," it shows you the science of why foods are good or bad for you, from insulin and antioxidants, to the fundamental chemistry of fats and vitamins. It starts you cooking healthy, delicious foods that kids will love, and shows you how to transition to a healthier lifestyle without causing mutiny in the ranks.

I have also written several articles for the site.

  • AngularJS
  • jQuery
  • HTMl
  • CSS
  • ASP.Net MVC

Knit Designer and Knit Designer Pro

This is a downloadable application for knitting pattern designers. Because every publisher in the knitting pattern industry thinks they are the "industry standard", they all put out different sizing requirements. This can be confusing and difficult for a new designer, not to mention time consuming.

Knit Designer allows the pattern designer to knit one garment, put in the measurements, and it generates a series of sizes and stitch numbers based on what publisher the designer chooses. It also generates a blocking diagram and has a charting system for cable and lace patterns.

Languages Used:
  • C#
